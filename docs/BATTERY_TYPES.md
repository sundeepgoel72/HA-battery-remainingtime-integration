# Battery Types

## Flooded Lead Acid

Traditional lead-acid batteries commonly used in backup power systems.

Characteristics:
- Lowest cost
- Requires maintenance
- Good calibration opportunities using resting voltage

## Tubular Lead Acid

Common in solar installations.

Characteristics:
- Deep cycle capable
- Long service life
- Recommended initial target profile

## AGM

Absorbent Glass Mat batteries.

Characteristics:
- Sealed
- Lower maintenance
- Lower internal resistance

## Gel

Gel electrolyte batteries.

Characteristics:
- Good deep cycling
- Sensitive to overcharging

## Lead Carbon

Enhanced lead-acid technology.

Characteristics:
- Better partial-state-of-charge operation
- Faster charging

## Custom Profiles

Future support for user-defined battery characteristics.
